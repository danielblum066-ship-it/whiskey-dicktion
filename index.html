About the Club

Whiskey Diction is a gathering of readers who appreciate both the slow burn of a good novel and the slow pour of a good dram. We meet every few months in rotating venues across Philadelphia, choose challenging and varied books, and settle in for the kind of long, wandering conversation that only happens when no one's in a hurry. Bring your copy, bring your opinions, and bring something worth sharing.
